  • Sky

    As an elementary school teacher, I often use Studio Ghibli movies in the classroom to teach children about the values of environmental protection, friendship, and courage. The natural beauty in "My Neighbor Totoro" makes children pay attention to every tree on campus; "Sky City" teaches them the importance of pursuing their dreams. These movies are more effective than any preaching

    Thank you for sharing

  • Crystal

    During my college years, I suffered from depression and was unable to live a normal life for a long time. One day, I stumbled upon "The Wind Rises" and was deeply moved by Cai Suizi's words, "It's so good to be alive." I started watching a movie by Hayao Miyazaki every day and gradually regained my love for life. Now, I have become an illustrator, hoping to use my paintbrush to convey this warmth to more people

    Thank you for sharing
