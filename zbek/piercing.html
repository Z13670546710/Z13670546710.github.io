Service Introduction

Explore different perforation locations to find the most suitable style for you. Click on the marked points on the image to learn more details.

Lobe

The most traditional and common perforation location has low pain and a short recovery period, making it suitable for first-time perforation patients.

Helix

The perforation located on the outer edge of the upper part of the ear can be arranged individually or in multiple ways, making it perfect for displaying small earrings or earrings.

Tragus

A small cartilage protrusion located at the entrance of the ear canal, which can be worn with small earrings after perforation, is fashionable and personalized.
